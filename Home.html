DOKO

Doko (डोको) is a Nepali word for a bamboo basket. As the bamboo basket reflects the beauty of sustainability, a reminder to honor and preserve the resources that nurture us, Doko is a one-stop portal where students can find all the resources they require throughout their four-year voyage here in Deerwalk Institute of Technology
